About ELS 300 Level

Welcome to the ELS 300 Level class page! This page provides information about the 300 level students of the Department of English and Literary Studies.

Our Journey

As third-year students, we delve deeper into the realm of English language and literature. Exploring advanced topics in syntax, linguistics, literature, and critical theory.

Program Highlights

  • Advanced courses in English syntax and lexis, linguistics, and literature.
  • In-depth analysis of African drama and African American literature.
  • Critical theory exploration for a deeper understanding of literary analysis.
  • Engaging oral literature projects to appreciate cultural narratives.

Meet Our Lecturers

Our dedicated and experienced lecturers guide us through our academic journey, fostering a passion for literature and linguistic analysis.
